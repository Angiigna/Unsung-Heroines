Laskarina Bouboulina

Laskarina Pinotsi, commonly known as Bouboulina, was a Greek naval commander in the Greek War of Independence in 1821, considered the first woman to attain the rank of admiral.

Lyudmila Pavlichenko

Lyudmila Mikhailovna Pavlichenko was a Soviet sniper in the Red Army during World War II, with a kill count of 309 she is considered one of the deadliest snipers in history. After she was injured in battle by a mortar shell, she was evacuated to Moscow.
